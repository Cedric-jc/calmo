Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Calmo ("we," "us," or "our") is a healing-focused social assistance app designed to help you navigate social conflicts with AI-powered suggestions. We are committed to a "Privacy-First" philosophy. This policy explains how we handle your information with maximum transparency and minimal data footprint.

Core Privacy Promise: We do not track you. We do not sell your data. Your conversations stay on your device, and we only process what is strictly necessary to provide our AI services.

2. Information Processing

To provide our core features, we process the following inputs:

  • User Inputs: This includes text you type, voice recordings, and images (chat screenshots) you upload for analysis.
  • AI Processing (Cloud-based): To generate intelligent reply suggestions, your inputs are encrypted and sent to our trusted cloud AI partners (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic). This data is processed in real-time and is not used for training AI models or stored permanently on our servers.
  • Local Storage: All your conversation history, generated responses, and diagnostic results are stored only on your local device using an encrypted local database (Hive). We do not maintain a remote database of your personal records.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

Unlike many other apps, Calmo maintains a strict "Zero Collection" policy for the following:

  • No Device Information: We do not collect your IMEI, IDFA, device model, or OS version.
  • No Location Data: We do not track your IP address or GPS location.
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not monitor how often you use the app or which buttons you click. Your app usage remains your own business.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption in Transit: Any data sent to the cloud for AI analysis is protected by industry-standard TLS/SSL encryption.
  • Physical Control: Since your data is stored locally, you have total physical control over it. Deleting a record in the app or uninstalling the app will permanently erase that data.

5. Your Rights and Control

Because your data resides on your device, you have the absolute right to:

  • Access & Export: View all your history directly within the app.
  • Deletion: Manually delete any specific entry or clear the entire database at any time.
  • Revoke Permissions: Stop data processing by disabling Microphone or Photo Library access in your system settings.

6. Third-Party Services

We only share your inputs with AI infrastructure providers necessary to generate responses. We do not partner with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing analytics firms.

7. Children's Privacy

Calmo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a minor, please use this service only under the supervision of a guardian.
