Calmo Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Calmo ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and related services ("Services").

Calmo is a healing-focused social assistance app that provides gentle reply suggestions through AI intent recognition to help users de-escalate social conflicts. Our core features include:

  • All-in-One Input Triangle: Support for text input, voice-to-text recognition, and image OCR for extracting chat content
  • AI Intent Recognition & Reply Generation: Local rule-based engine that identifies 6 intent types (humor, empathy, rejection, professional, conflict, breakup) and generates responses
  • Conversation Lab: Provides comprehensive diagnostics, simulation sandbox, safety assessment, and psychology suggestions
  • Inspiration Chronicle: Automatically saves all generated replies with search, filter, and batch operation support

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us:

  • Conversation Content: Text inputs, voice recordings, and images you upload for analysis and response generation
  • OCR Data: Extracted text from images (chat screenshots) for intent analysis
  • Conversation History: Generated responses, intent tags, and analysis results stored in your local archive
  • Settings and Preferences: OCR preferences, theme settings, notification preferences, and customization options
  • Lab Analysis Data: Conversation diagnostics, intent classifications, safety assessments, and psychology suggestions from the Conversation Lab feature
  • Feedback: Your responses to surveys, feature requests, and support inquiries

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Usage Data: Features you use, time spent in the app, interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information
  • Location Data: General location information (country/region) based on IP address (not precise location)

2.3 Third-Party Information

We may receive information from third-party services:

  • App Store Providers: Download and installation information
  • Analytics Providers: Aggregated usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Cloud Service Providers: Infrastructure and storage services

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Data Storage

Calmo uses a privacy-first approach to data storage:

  • Local Storage: Your conversation history, generated responses, and settings are stored locally on your device using Hive database (encrypted local storage)
  • Minimal Cloud Storage: Only anonymized usage analytics and crash reports are transmitted to secure servers for service improvement
  • OCR Settings: You can configure OCR to extract only opponent's chat bubbles and automatically delete original images after processing

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) for any data transmission
  • Local encryption for sensitive data stored on device
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

6.2 Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our Services and for legitimate business purposes:

  • Conversation History & Archive: Stored locally on your device until you manually delete them or uninstall the app
  • Lab Analysis Data: Stored locally until you delete the specific analysis or your conversation history
  • Settings & Preferences: Stored locally until you change them or uninstall the app
  • Usage Analytics: 12 months (anonymized after 6 months) for cloud-transmitted data
  • Support Records: 24 months

After the retention period expires, we will securely delete or anonymize your data. For locally stored data, you have full control over deletion through the app's archive management features.

6.3 Security Measures

Important: While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
