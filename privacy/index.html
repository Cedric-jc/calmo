Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Calmo - Warmly Connected ("we," "us," or "our") is a healing-focused social assistance app designed to help you navigate social conflicts with AI-powered suggestions. We are committed to a "Privacy-First" philosophy. This policy explains how we handle your information with maximum transparency and minimal data footprint.

Core Privacy Promise: We do not sell your data. You have full control: choose Local Mode to keep data only on your device, or Cloud Mode for secure backup and sync. We only process what is strictly necessary to provide our services.

2. Information Collection & Processing

To provide our services, we process the following data based on your usage mode:

  • Account Information (Optional): If you create an account, we collect your email address and set up a secure authentication token. This allows you to log in across multiple devices.
  • User Inputs: Text, voice, and images you provide for analysis.
  • Data Storage Options:
    • Guest Mode: All data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to it, and it is lost if you uninstall the app.
    • Logged-in Mode: Your conversation history and settings are encrypted and stored on our secure cloud servers to enable backup and synchronization.
  • AI Processing: Inputs are sent to our AI partners (e.g., OpenAI) solely for generating responses. We strictly prohibit our partners from using your data to train their models.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

We maintain a strict minimization policy:

  • No Ad Tracking: We do not collect IDFA or Advertising IDs.
  • No Precise Location: We do not track your GPS location.
  • No Sale of Data: We never sell your personal information or conversation content to third parties.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption Everywhere: Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256) on our servers for logged-in users.
  • Access Control: Our employees are strictly prohibited from accessing your private conversation content unless legally required.

5. Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Access & Export: You can view or export your data at any time.
  • Delete Account: You can delete your account via Settings. This will permanently erase your personal info and cloud data from our servers.
  • Switch Modes: You can log out to switch to Guest Mode (note: cloud data will no longer sync).

6. Third-Party Services

We only share your inputs with AI infrastructure providers necessary to generate responses. We do not partner with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing analytics firms.

7. Children's Privacy

Calmo - Warmly Connected is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a minor, please use this service only under the supervision of a guardian.
